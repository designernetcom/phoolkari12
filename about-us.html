About Us

Welcome to Phoolkari Wellness LLP

Is a purpose-driven company rooted
in Indian traditions and committed to sustainable innovation.

Is a purpose-driven company rooted in Indian traditions and committed to sustainable innovation. We specialize in eco-friendly products crafted from sacred floral offerings collected from temples, transforming spiritual waste into meaningful wellness items like incense sticks, organic compost, and herbal goods.

Our flagship initiative turns temple flowers into charcoal-free agarbattis (incense sticks) under unique spiritual brands like Swami Krupa, Trimbekraj Krupa, and more—offering devotees a way to bring home divine blessings in an eco-conscious form.

Our Mission

➡︎ Reverence to Resources* To respectfully collect and repurpose floral offerings (Nirmalya) from temples into eco-friendly products, preserving spiritual sanctity and environmental harmony.
➡︎ Eco-Innovation To deploy green technologies such as solar dryers and biodegradable packaging for creating safe products.

Our Vision

➡︎ To become a global leader in spiritual sustainability by transforming sacred waste into holistic wellness products that honor tradition, uplift communities, and heal the planet.*
➡︎ Reconnect People with Inner Peace To inspire moments of stillness and mindfulness through natural, handcrafted incense that nurtures the soul and calms the mind.

Our History

Our journey began with
a simple intention

To create incense that feels pure, honest, and connected to nature. What started as a small passion project in a humble workshop has now grown into a community-centered effort to share the healing power of natural aromas with the world.
Inspired by traditional methods and sacred rituals, we began crafting incense by hand—using only natural herbs, resins, wood powders, and essential oils.
