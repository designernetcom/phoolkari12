    natural air freshener that promotes clarity and peace of mind

    Sandalwood incense is revered for its warm, woody, and creamy aroma that gently lingers in the air, creating a serene and meditative environment.

  • Lavender

    Ideal for relaxing, creating a calm environment in homes

    Lavender incense offers a light, floral, herbal aroma noted for its mooduplifting and stressreducing properties. It has long been used as an antidepressant and refreshing home fragrance.

  • Nag Champa

    Includes floral powders, woody resins, essential oils, and spices.

    One of the most iconic Indian incense fragrances, Nag Champa blends sandalwood with champa or frangipani flowers for a rich, earthy-sweet, slightly spicy scent.

  • Frankincense & Myrrh

    Enhances rituals, meditation, and sacred space creation.

    This classic duo has been used in religious and spiritual ceremonies for millennia. Frankincense brings a balsamic, citrusy-woody fragrance, while myrrh adds deep, resinous, slightly earthy and bitter-sweet undertones.

  • Rose

    Perfect for relaxation, love-themed rituals, gentle meditation

    Rose incense captures the timeless elegance of blooming roses, delivering a sweet, romantic, and luxurious fragrance. Its perfect for setting a calming and loving atmosphere, whether you're meditating, relaxing, or simply enhancing your living space.

  • Patchouli

    Commonly used in aromatherapy, spiritual rituals

    Patchouli incense offers a deep, earthy, and slightly sweet aroma with a musky undertone that is both sensual and grounding. Often associated with the bohemian and spiritual movements of the 1960s, it remains a favorite for those seeking connection with nature
